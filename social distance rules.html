Social distance rules

1-Make sure to keep 2m social distance from people

2-You will be required to wear face masks when you leave the car

3-There will be hand sanitiser stations at the venue

4-don't leave your car unless you need to use the toilet facilities or to collect your hot food order.

5-Respecting the social distancing guidelines and sticking to the designated paths.
